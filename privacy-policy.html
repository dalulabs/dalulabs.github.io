Privacy Policy for Kalata

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Kalata (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is a collaborative shopping-list app published by Dalu. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and your rights over it. By using Kalata you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — if you create an account: your email address, a password (stored encrypted by our authentication provider; we never see it in plain text), and a display name you choose.
  • Guest accounts — if you use the app as a guest, we create an anonymous identifier so your lists work on your device. No email or name is collected unless you later create an account.
  • Your content — the shopping lists and items you create (names, quantities, categories, completion status) and, for shared lists, which accounts are members.
  • Share codes — temporary codes you generate to invite others to a list.

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, or payment information. Kalata contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

2. How we use your information

Your information is used solely to provide the app’s functionality: authenticating you, storing and syncing your lists across your devices, and enabling list sharing between members. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

3. How your data is stored

Kalata uses Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) as its backend. Your data is stored on Google’s cloud infrastructure and processed according to Google’s privacy and security terms. Access is restricted by security rules so that only members of a list can read or modify it.

4. Sharing your information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With our infrastructure provider (Google Firebase) strictly to operate the app.
  • With other members of a list you have chosen to share — they can see that list’s contents and that you are a member.
  • Where required by law.

5. Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time, directly in the app: Settings → Delete Account. This removes your account, the lists you own and their items, and your membership from any shared lists. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

6. Children

Kalata is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, announced in the app.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact us at dalulabs.app@gmail.com.
