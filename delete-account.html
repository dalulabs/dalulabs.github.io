Delete your account & data

Kalata: Shopping List — published by Dalu

Kalata lets you permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can do it yourself inside the app, or request it by email.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open Kalata and go to the Settings tab.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and data are deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request by email

Email dalulabs.app@gmail.com from the address linked to your account, with the subject "Delete my account". We will delete your account and data within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email address, display name, and authentication record)
  • All shopping lists and items you own
  • Your membership in any lists shared with you
  • Any share codes you created

What is kept, and for how long

  • Nothing that personally identifies you is retained after deletion.
  • In-app deletions are processed immediately; email requests are completed within 30 days.

You can also delete individual lists or items at any time inside the app, without deleting your whole account.

Questions? Contact dalulabs.app@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
